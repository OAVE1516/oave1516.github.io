What is BlockParty?

BlockParty is an up-and-coming, exciting new company dedicated to meeting all your party needs and helping you host the party of your dreams by providing instant parties at the pull of a string. With our staff of 32 dedicated, well-trained employees, we have all the means necessary to execute the perfect party. We have an extensive array of options available to address your needs. We serve all regions of the United States and can ship our products nationally. We also offer customizable options such as themes, decorations, catering, and more. BlockParty is constantly improving our products by offering new themes originating from modern art forms, such as literature, movies, and music, as well as improving the design and execution of our products to increase efficiency and enjoyment for our customers.

Our Solution

Our solution to solving the issue of wasted hours and effort is allowing our customers to customize every aspect of their party online, then sending them a BlockParty which they set up in the middle of the room where the party will be held. The remote that comes along with the block will set up and clean up the party using Presto! technology. When they are customizing their party online, our customers pick who and what they want at their party, and we do the work. It's that simple.

Mission Statement

To deliver the complete, celebratory experience through extensive personalization and innovative technology to make our customers' lives more efficient and enjoyable.
